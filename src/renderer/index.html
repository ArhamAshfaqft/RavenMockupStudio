Studio Expansion Update

  • Cloud Store: Browse and instantly download hundreds of new premium mockups directly inside the app.

  • Linked Folders: Link custom folders from your PC straight into the library sidebar without moving any files.
  • Alignment Tools: Snap your designs to center or fill-width with new single-click action buttons.

  • Enhanced Realism: Upgraded rendering slider allowing for deeper shadows and stronger textures.
  • Library Refinement: Categories reorganized into a flat, interactive sidebar for faster navigation.
  • Expanded Content: Added high-demand categories including Jerseys, Baby Onesies, and Faceless Models.
